About

My Story

Hi! My name is Sukhleen and I am a UX Designer based in NYC. I'm a bit of a jack of all trades with an undergraduate degree in English, a graduate degree in Education, and a love of the arts.

I love R&B music, all things sci-fi, film, writing, singing, and traveling! I tend to be a bit of a type A person when it comes to routines and organization, but I also enjoy taking the occasional risk to favor spontaneity and feed my insatiable curiosity about the world around me.

I've always had a passion for helping and understanding others, empathy being one of my core values. In my previous role as a Special Education teacher, through leading with empathy and understanding, I was able to support my students' academic growth to the best of my ability. On a day-to-day basis I effectively communicated student needs and goals to co-teachers, modified and adapted lesson plans to fit individual needs of students, and used creativity to make learning fun and more digestible.

As a UX Designer, I hope to bring my teacher mindset and skills into my new workspace. I hope to continue to empathize, be communicative, problem solve, and explore creativity in my new role as a designer!
